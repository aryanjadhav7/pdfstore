# PDF Store

A curated collection of industry-specific questionnaire and application forms for various business domains.

## Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive set of PDF documents designed for businesses and organizations across multiple sectors. These questionnaires and forms serve as templates for client intake, risk assessment, and application processes.

## Document Categories

### Automotive & Transportation
- **Antique Classic Auto Restoration Questionnaire** - Form for antique vehicle restoration services
- **Auto Auction Questionnaire** - Client intake form for auto auction operations
- **Heavy Vehicle Questionnaire** - Assessment form for heavy vehicle operations
- **Motorcycle Off Road Questionnaire** - Specialized form for motorcycle and off-road vehicle businesses
- **Wholesale Dealer Questionnaire** - Application form for wholesale vehicle dealers
- **Towing Operations Questionnaire** (GAR SUP135) - Risk assessment for towing services
- **RV Questionnaire** (GAR SUP132) - Form for recreational vehicle operations

### Hospitality & Entertainment
- **Bars and Restaurants** - Comprehensive questionnaire for food and beverage establishments
- **Security Guard Call Center** - Application and assessment form for security operations

### Childcare & Education
- **Child Day Care and Preschool** - Questionnaire for childcare facility licensing
- **Commercial Child Care** (Dapp) - Professional childcare operation form
- **Daycare Assessment** (Ategrity) - Facility assessment and evaluation form

### Specialty Services & Retail
- **Boat Watercraft Questionnaire** - Form for marine vessel operations and sales
- **Mobility Equipment Sales Questionnaire** - Assessment for mobility device retailers
- **Salvage Yard Questionnaire** - Operational form for auto salvage facilities
- **Wholesale Distributor Application** - Form for wholesale distribution businesses

### Products & Services
- **Great American Products Application** - Generic application form for product-based businesses

## Usage

These documents are intended to be used as:
- **Templates** for creating your own business forms
- **Reference materials** for understanding industry-standard questionnaires
- **Intake forms** for client onboarding processes
- **Risk assessment documents** for insurance and compliance purposes

## How to Use

1. Review the relevant questionnaire for your industry
2. Customize the form to match your specific business needs
3. Use as a template for client intake or application processes
4. Ensure compliance with local, state, and federal regulations
